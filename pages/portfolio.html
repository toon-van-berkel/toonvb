AnimeShow

AnimeShow is a sort of replacement for 9anime. With 9anime you constantly get popups and ads everywhere you go. With Animeshow the tought is that you get 1 ad at the beginning of a episode or show that lasts for 30 seconds. But for movies 1 minute. This way the website stays usable and easy to navigate.
