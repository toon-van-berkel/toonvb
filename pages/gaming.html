About Me / Gaming

Gaming Journey

For Toon van Berkel, gaming is far more than just a way to pass the time — it’s a meaningful escape, a source of creativity, and a path to new connections. With every game played, Toon has discovered valuable skills, unforgettable moments, and personal growth.

Gaming as a Creative Outlet: Toon’s journey with Minecraft began as a child, where he used the game to express imagination through limitless building. Whether designing entire cities or experimenting with redstone mechanics, it remains a favorite platform for creativity and innovation.

Cooperation & Competitive Spirit: In Fortnite, Toon found a perfect balance between fun and competition. Playing alongside friends, completing missions, and unlocking rare skins became a thrilling cycle of achievement — which is how his collection grew to over 250 skins.

Multiplayer gaming has also helped Toon sharpen real-world skills in communication, adaptability, and teamwork. Whether coordinating with others in strategy games or exploring survival-based titles, each session has reinforced valuable life lessons.

Gaming as Reflection: Looking back on these experiences, Toon recognizes how each game has left a lasting mark. They’ve challenged him to think critically, stay persistent, and embrace continuous learning. Gaming has not only entertained but educated and inspired.

Today, Toon continues to explore immersive game worlds, discover new titles, and appreciate the evolving art of interactive storytelling. For him, gaming remains a space to unwind, recharge, and stay connected to both creativity and community.
