About Toon van Berkel – Software Developer, Tech Enthusiast & Creative Explorer

Who is Toon van Berkel?

A Journey into IT

Toon van Berkel’s fascination with IT began at an early age. While still in primary school, he wanted to play Minecraft with friends—which led him to build a server from scratch on his personal PC. That moment sparked a lifelong passion for coding, learning, and solving complex problems through technology. With every line of code, Toon continues to explore the endless potential of digital creation.

A Passion for Travel

Traveling is one of Toon’s greatest inspirations. Having explored many countries already, his ultimate goal is to visit every nation in the world. With every trip, Toon gains new perspectives, cultural insights, and stories that continue to shape both his worldview and his work.

Building Connections

Toon believes in the power of community. Whether it's through scouting, travel, online collaboration, or tech events—he enjoys meeting new people, exchanging ideas, and building a strong professional network. His friendly and open nature makes him approachable and easy to work with.

JavaScript
