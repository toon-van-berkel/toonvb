Portfolio / ElectroVibe

ElectroVibe – Modern Webshop

ElectroVibe is a feature-rich webshop developed during Toon van Berkel’s fourth study period, in collaboration with Vince Apeldoorn. You can view his portfolio here.

This project emphasizes both technical capability and user experience. From category-based product navigation to secure login and admin panels, every component was designed with usability and performance in mind. ElectroVibe represents their shared commitment to quality development and smart design.

Design Approach

The ElectroVibe design is clean, responsive, and user-focused. Whether browsing products or navigating between pages, users are guided through a clear, engaging interface. The visual consistency and mobile optimization ensure a seamless experience across all screen sizes.

Accessibility and responsiveness were key design goals. Each element was crafted with clarity and usability in mind, offering a professional look while being easy to use.

Development & Code

Behind the simple interface lies a robust backend, featuring secure authentication, product management, and admin tools. Special attention was given to protecting sensitive data and managing user interactions securely.

Toon van Berkel and Vince implemented efficient frontend logic while maintaining backend scalability. Technologies such as Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and JWT were used to ensure security, speed, and maintainability.
