My Chemical Romance – Fan Website Redesign

This project is a fan-made redesign of the official My Chemical Romance website, created by Toon van Berkel to modernize the user experience and provide a cleaner, more intuitive interface.

The site layout includes distinct pages for the band, music, gallery, news, events, and merchandise. Each section is visually separated and optimized for usability across desktop and mobile devices.
