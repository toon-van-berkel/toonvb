About Me / Traveling

Exploring the World with Toon van Berkel

Toon van Berkel has a deep passion for travel and discovering new cultures. His goal is to one day visit every country, capturing experiences that shape his view of the world and fuel his creativity. Traveling is more than just a hobby—it’s a source of joy, learning, and unforgettable memories.

Mexico

Mexico left a lasting impression on Toon van Berkel. He admired the warmth of the people, the beauty of the landscapes, and the pleasant weather. It was a journey filled with surprises and authentic culture.

Portugal

From the vibrant streets of Lisbon to the peaceful shores of the Algarve, Portugal offered Toon van Berkel a perfect blend of coastal beauty, rich history, and delicious cuisine. It was a destination that truly captivated him.

Kenya

Kenya offered Toon van Berkel a breathtaking experience with its vast savannahs, majestic wildlife, and the iconic Great Rift Valley. The journey was both humbling and unforgettable—rich in nature and culture.
