Vakantie Mexico

My trip to Mexico was beautiful. I loved how social and welcoming the people were. Even though many did not have much themselves, they were always willing to help. At times, we expected people to ask for money for their help, but they never did and simply continued with their day, happy to assist. It was a huge contrast to what we had anticipated.
