Mexico – A Journey of Kindness

Toon van Berkel's trip to Mexico was filled with vibrant culture, warm weather, and heartfelt hospitality. What stood out most wasn’t just the scenery—it was the people. Kindness flowed naturally from locals, even when they had little to give. Acts of generosity came freely, without expectation or reward.

Whether offering help on the street or providing directions, strangers were genuinely kind. The trip served as a reminder of how impactful small gestures can be and how powerful human connection is across cultures.
