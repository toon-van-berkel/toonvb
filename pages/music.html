About Me / Music

Music That Inspires

For many years, Toon van Berkel preferred silence over sound — until he discovered how deeply music could speak to emotion, energy, and focus. What began as disinterest evolved into a powerful connection with bands whose music conveys purpose, power, and personal meaning.

The Role of Music

From the timeless voices of Queen and The Beatles to the raw intensity of Slipknot and the stage power of Rammstein, Toon draws energy and clarity from every track. Whether he’s writing code or reflecting on life, music enhances his process. Songs become markers of emotion, memory, and momentum — often influencing his workflow and creative mindset.
