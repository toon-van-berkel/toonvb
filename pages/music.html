About Me / Music

Music

I started out disliking music, preferring silence over sound. However, as time passed, I began to appreciate the beauty and complexity within it. Today, music is a source of inspiration and energy, with a variety that spans genres and stories, each carrying a deeper meaning than we often realize.

What Music Means to Me

From classic rock legends like Queen and The Beatles to the powerful sounds of Slipknot and Rammstein, each of these bands brings a unique vibe. Their music inspires me, gives me energy, and even helps me focus while coding. Every song has a memory attached to it, whether it's the adrenaline of an AC/DC riff or the intricate lyrics of Avenged Sevenfold.
