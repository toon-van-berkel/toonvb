About Toon van Berkel – Software Developer, Tech Visionary & Creative Thinker

Who is Toon van Berkel?

From Minecraft to Mastery: The Start of a Tech Journey

Toon van Berkel discovered his passion for technology early in life. As a curious primary school student eager to play Minecraft with friends, he built his own game server from scratch. This hands-on experience ignited a lifelong journey into the world of programming, digital innovation, and creative problem-solving. Today, Toon continues to refine his skills with every project—driven by a relentless curiosity and a love for exploring what's possible through technology.

Exploring the World, Expanding Horizons

For Toon, travel is more than a hobby—it’s a source of inspiration and growth. He has already explored a wide range of countries, each journey enriching his perspective and fueling his creativity. His dream of visiting every country in the world is not just about places, but about the stories, cultures, and connections he discovers along the way.

Tech Meets Community: A Passion for People

Beyond code, Toon values connection. Active in scouting, collaborative projects, and tech communities, he thrives on meaningful interactions and shared learning experiences. His approachable personality, team spirit, and commitment to growth make him a valued collaborator in both personal and professional settings.
